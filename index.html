Stories Together Terms of Service

Stories Together is a Discord bot made to facilitate storymaking. Use the bot as you see fit, as long as you do not violate Discord's Terms of Service.

Stories Together Privacy Policy

This bot stores the username and ID of users who submit a story prompt or story entry, as well as the prompt or entry submitted. It also stores the ID of each guild it's in, as well as the discord channel ID a user sets the bot to use. This data is stored via Google's Firebase Database.

Members of a Discord server you submit prompts/passages in may view the contributions you've made to a story, and see it as attributed to your username.

To request removal of your data, you may contact "lego" via Discord direct message or use the bot's "/story feedback" command.
